Who is the Spy

🎉 Welcome to Who is the Spy! 🕵️‍♀️

Get ready for the ultimate party game where deception and deduction collide! Can you figure out who the Spy is before it’s too late? Let’s find out!

How to Play:

  1. Set the Scene: Enter the number of players (3–20) and click “Generate Cards.” Each card has a word... except one. Someone is the Spy!
  2. How Many Spies? The number of Spies depends on the total players:
    • 3–5 players: 1 Spy
    • 6–10 players: 2 Spies
    • 11–20 players: 3 Spies
  3. Keep It Secret: Click your card to see your word—don’t let anyone else peek!
  4. What’s the Deal? Take turns giving clues about your word. If you’re the Spy, fake it like your life depends on it!
  5. The Big Reveal: Accuse, debate, and vote to uncover the Spy. Will they escape undetected?
  6. Victory: If the group catches the Spy, they win! If not, the Spy gets the last laugh!

Pro Tips:

  • Be clever but subtle with your clues.
  • Spies, bluff confidently—confusion is your ally!
  • Trust no one. Seriously, no one.

Ready to find the Spy? Let the games begin! 🎭
