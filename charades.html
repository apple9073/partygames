Charades

🎉 Welcome to Charades! 🎭

Get ready for a fun and fast-paced acting game! You'll have a limited time to act out as many words as possible. Can you beat the clock? Let’s find out!

How to Play:

  1. Set the Timer: Enter the number of minutes (1–5) for the round and click “Start Timer.”
  2. Act It Out: A word will appear on the screen. Act it out without speaking!
  3. Success or Pass: If your team guesses the word correctly, click "Success." If not, click "Pass" to skip to the next word.
  4. Keep Going: The game continues until the timer runs out.
  5. Final Score: At the end of the round, you'll see how many words you successfully guessed!

Pro Tips:

  • Be creative with your acting—no speaking allowed!
  • Work as a team to guess as many words as possible.
  • Have fun and don't stress—it's all about the laughs!
